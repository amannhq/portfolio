## what works

after years of experimentation, i landed on a simple approach:

### capture fast

when something's interesting, write it down immediately. don't organize, don't format. just capture.

```markdown
# 2026-01-25
- learned about X
- idea: Y could work for Z
- link: https://...
```

### review weekly

once a week, spend 15 minutes:

- move important stuff to permanent notes
- delete noise
- connect related ideas

### keep it plain text

markdown files in a folder. that's it. no fancy apps, no sync issues, no lock-in.
